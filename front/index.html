How does Measure My Size work?

Measure My Size uses advanced AI to analyse two photographs you upload. It then accurately calculates 10 key body measurements and creates a digital avatar matching your body shape. This process is quick, easy, and eliminates the need for traditional measuring tapes.

Is Measure My Size easy to use for beginners?

Absolutely! Our service is designed with user-friendliness in mind. Just upload two photos following our simple guidelines, and our system takes care of the rest. You don't need any technical or fashion expertise to use Measure My Size.

Are my photographs and data secure with Measure My Size?

Yes, your privacy and security are paramount to us. We use advanced encryption and security protocols to ensure your photos and data are protected and confidential.

Can I adjust the measurements provided by Measure My Size?

Yes, you can! We understand that everyone has unique preferences for fit. Our tool allows you to tweak certain measurements to suit your desired fit, ensuring your clothes are tailored just the way you like them.
